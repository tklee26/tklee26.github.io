PhD Student in Economics

Boston University

I am a 4th year economics PhD student at Boston University. My research interests primarily lie in the intersection of industrial organization and financial markets. I use tools from industrial organization to better understand how different segments of financial markets operate. My current research project focuses on network formation in the US financial brokerage industry.

Prior to my PhD journey, I completed my undergraduate studies in economics and mathematics at Boston College.
